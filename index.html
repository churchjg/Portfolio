About Me

I attended James Madison University for my undergraduate degree, attaining a B.S. in Sports Management before getting an M.Ed in Higher Education from the University of Virginia. After working for a little over two years in the collegiate athletics industry, I moved to Arlington, VA and found it was the perfect time for a career change. I attended General Assembly's Software Engineering Immersive program. I am now a Full Stack Software Engineer and am looking to direct my work ethic towards rewarding and challenging projects. I am motivated by continuous growth, advancing communities through technology, and learning new things. My adaptability and passion for learning make me a great fit for any organization.
